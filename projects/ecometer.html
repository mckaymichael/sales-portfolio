EcoMeter

The Problem

Restaurants unknowingly bleed profits through unchecked operational waste, contributing directly to the environmental crisis of commercial landfill disposal.

Project Abstract

"Trust in EcoMeter today. Achieve zero waste tomorrow."

Unique Value Proposition

Serving as both a sustainability consultancy and a public ranking system. We turn an environmental problem into a financial benefit by gathering operational waste data to pinpoint areas where restaurants can maximize savings.

Impact Metrics & SDGs

2% Cut in Food Cost SDG 2: Zero Hunger SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

The Mission

To tackle waste in the food industry by proving that at EcoMeter we hate waste, and you should too. So, let's avoid it.

The Vision

To help society achieve zero waste tomorrow and to build partnerships between restaurants and nonprofits by 2027 to tackle the UN’s Zero Hunger goal.

About EcoMeter

EcoMeter conducts comprehensive two-week waste audits for local food and beverage providers. By meticulously tracking beginning inventory, incoming purchases, and ending inventory, we identify massive inefficiencies that quietly drain restaurant profits.

During this audit, our team installs categorized bins and specialized scales directly on-site to accurately track spoilage, prep waste, and plate waste. We evaluate how locally a restaurant shops to mitigate community waste miles, and aggressively review packaging based on local Metro Vancouver recycling capabilities—looking past misleading manufacturer claims.

Following the rigorous audit, restaurants are given a grace period to implement tangible sustainable practices and transition to green suppliers. Once complete, their verified EcoMeter score is made public through a customizable website widget, turning operational efficiency into a transparent, front-facing marketing asset for patrons who care about the environment.
